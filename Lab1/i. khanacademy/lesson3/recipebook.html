Smoothie

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
milk 350 ml
yoghurt 250 ml
banana 1
strawberry 15

Step 1: Blitz the strawberries in a blender with the banana and orange juice until smooth.

Step 2: Pour the smoothie into a tall glass to serve.

Chicken Pastina

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
chicken broth 4 cup
pastina 1 cup
salt 5 g
butter 2 spoons

Step 1: Bring broth to a simmer over medium-high heat. Taste; season with salt if needed.

Step 2: Ladle pastina into hot bowls. Top with more freshly ground black pepper and remaining Parmigiano Reggiano; drizzle with olive oil and serve.

Pie cookies

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
pie crust 1
egg 1
sugar 2 soopn
milk 1 cup

Step 1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line 2 large rimmed baking sheets with parchment paper; set aside.

Step 2: Roll premade pie crust to 1/8-inch thickness on a lightly floured work surface, and cut using desired cookie cutter shapes.

Step 3: Transfer cut-outs to prepared baking sheets, leaving about a 1/2-inch space between each cookie. Repeat with remaining dough, rerolling scraps once
