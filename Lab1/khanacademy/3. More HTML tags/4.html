Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Eggs 3
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Pancetta 100g
Black Pepper To taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  3. Fry pancetta until crispy, then add to the cooked spaghetti.
  4. Mix the egg mixture with the spaghetti off the heat.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Butter 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 1.5 cups

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add flour and mix until dough forms.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Romaine Lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Caesar Dressing 1/2 cup
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. Toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing.
  3. Add croutons and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!
